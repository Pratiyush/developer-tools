# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the **Contributor Covenant 2.1**.

The full text of the covenant is available at:

- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/
- https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/code_of_conduct.md

In short: be respectful, assume good intent, disagree on substance not on people, and help keep this a welcoming space for contributors of every background and experience level.

## Reporting

To report behavior that violates the covenant, contact the project maintainer:

**Pratiyush Kumar** — `pratiyush1@gmail.com`

All reports are reviewed and handled confidentially. Reporters will not be retaliated against.

## Enforcement

The maintainer follows the **Enforcement Guidelines** documented in Contributor Covenant 2.1 (Correction → Warning → Temporary Ban → Permanent Ban). The full enforcement matrix is at the URLs above.
